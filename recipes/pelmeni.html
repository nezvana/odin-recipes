Pelmeni Recipe

Pelmeni are traditional Russian dumplings filled with meat. Here's how to make them:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, water, egg, and salt to make the dough. Knead until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix the ground pork, ground beef, chopped onion, salt, and pepper to make the filling.
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a round cutter to cut out circles of dough.
  4. Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape and pinch the edges to seal.
  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pelmeni and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until they float to the surface.
  6. Remove the pelmeni with a slotted spoon and serve hot with sour cream or butter.

Enjoy your homemade pelmeni!
